June 2017

The First BGSMUN

The first cohort of BGS MUNners got together to conduct the first MUN in this school. This intra-school MUN served to train the next cohort.

June 2019

BGSMUN'19

After a one-year hiatus, recognizing the need to train a new set of students; we conducted the second BGSMUN.

July 2019

Best Delegation at DiPSMUN'19

After many misses and the passing out of BGS's finest MUNner yet, we won the best delegation award at DiPSMUN'19 conducted by DPS Bangalore South

July 24 and 25, 2021

BGSMUN'21

After a year where the world was turned upside-down, BGSMUN returns, in a new online format. The goal remains same: expose a new batch to MUN. This time, we expand to 4 committees.
